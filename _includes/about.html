Company background

Cultivating Young Mind focuses on youth guidance in career choices they follow ultimately.
It was founded two dynamic and talented Black women Esme Bhengu & Daisy Pheme who are driven and passionate about education and the youth of South Africa in general. Furthermore, they are passionate about assisting young people to follow careers of their dreams.
Esme is a Supply chain professional, who holds a Bcom Supply Chain from University of Natal (Pietermaritzburg Campus) and she later enrolled for Management advanced Program (Wits Business School).
Esme Bhengu has over 15 year experience in Supply Chain and has worked for different organization, including FMCG, Petroleum, Retail & Banking sector. Her vast business experienced has enabled her to understand different careers in different industries.
Daisy holds a degree in Sport Management and MTech Marketing from Tshwane University of Technology. She is currently lecturing at Tshwane University of Technology.
Both founders are qualified Career Practioners in SA. They have also done an extensive benchmarking on career guidance globally, which allows Cultivating Young Mind to successfully offer an individually designed advice to its clients.
